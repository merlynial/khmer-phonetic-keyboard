Signs & punctuation

Clusters / subscript (coeng ្): just type consonants together with no vowel between — skl→ស្ក្ល, knhom→ក្ញុំ. To force a break instead of stacking, put a space or a . between them.
Inherent vowel: a consonant alone carries the built-in “â/o” sound. k→ក, ka→ក (the a adds nothing).
Series shifters: *=៉ (musikatoan: m*aong→ម៉ោង), `=៊ (treisap: s`→ស៊).
Capitals matter: d=ដ but D=ឌ, n=ន but N=ណ, l=ល but L=ឡ.
